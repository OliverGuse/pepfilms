FILM SYNOPSIS -
The Philippine 1970’s rock band “Don’t Stop”, three groupies along with an AWOL Soldier venture to a remote island in the Philippines. The unfortunate band of seven youths plan for peace, love, rock & roll. All the innocent group wants is a wild time but they find a primal fight for life but Everything was captured on 16mm film and found 40 years later. 

A documentary team relentlessly investigates the vintage found footage and the cover up. The team chases down every clue and the powers that be. Be apart of a new type of film, production and genera - AUDITION NOW!

- Audition Process -

Record the following on your phone, post online and email us the link with a short introduction. CC’ the audition of your manager or agent.  If you are a under 18 you must cc’ your parents with a permission letter from them to audition. Minors parents will be involved in the contract process and personally co-sign the contract. Do not worry if you have no agent or  manager please audition anyways. Talented people right for the roles will be favored. Contact - casting@pepfilms.com . 

  1. Sing The Beatles Song Obladi Oblada - have a sing a long or put an original twist on the song. Do not worry you do not need to be Celine Dion but have fun!  (1 to 2 mins video only)  Song Link 
  2. DO ANY ACTION WHILE TALKING ABOUT YOURSELF -  Do something while talking about yourself. For an example: Drink a cup of coffee, go for a walk, fry an egg and tell us a story, secret or anything about yourself. Its up to you what you DO AND SAY at the same time. (3 to 5 mins video only) Do & Say   
  3. BE AFRAID... REALLY AFRAID - Find a dark corner inside or out and be terrified but trying to stay quiet or they will find you. You are so scared this is difficult to do but you must video a message. The scene can be robbery, killer loose or kidnapers looking for you and you cannot get away. Improvise its all about control, emotion and fear. (3 to 5 mins video only) Acting Link

- Production Information -

Please note this is a personal passion project and an Independent project and the fees and terms are non negotiable do not apply if you are not okay with the following terms, vision and fees. We are blessed to have a budget but it is limited.

  1. Producing for exposure -  Any and all promotion, marketing, press releases, festivals, junkets, advertising, campaigns, media, interviews. Festivals and any and all exposure is our main focus for all involved. All of the above will be contractually shared to better everyone’s future endeavors the best possible. This film is to be produced to the highest level and quality possible any other motivation would nullify the above primary drive.
  2. Schedule - Filming 4 to 6 weeks. 4 week schedule plus a 2 week buffer in-case of issues to better the a film. 6 day work week and Sunday rest days.
  3. Internet - Everyone involved will have 2 hours a day access to the internet x 2 times per daytotaling 4 hours. This is a period piece and it is important you we must live in the moment for the betterment of the film and the performances. INCASE OF EMERGENCY 24/7 ACCESS IS PROVIDED TO EVERYONE.
  4. Privacy - No production pictures, or information of any kind will be posted, shared or released until after the films official release of the film to anyone. To increase the impact of the film and the impact your debut in the global film industry.
  5. Trust - This film will take a high level of commitment and trust between the artist, director and the production team. Any and all concerns should be communicated. We will have a qualified therapist and point person on staff to talk to 24/7 and the director/producer is available at all times.
  6. Unorthodox Process - The director ONLY has your interest at stake and therefore the film as well. This film it is of the upmost importance to believe in the unorthodox process and directing style. This method is in the interest of the actors, the performance and the film. We are producing this film to showcase your talent make you look good and intern making a great movie.
  7. Set - All talent and crew will be treated as equals in this production and team bonding will ensure everyone's success. This film is a chance of a lifetime for all involved and we need a higher level of team work.
  8. Food - All food request, restrictions and allergies should be made in advance.  We will do our best to make the set as enjoyable as possible with the best food and drinks possible.
  9. Re-numeration - 10,000 peso per week on set plus food, lodging, communication and transportation for all 7 ensemble cast members during production. 1,000 per day for all pre-production and post-production, training, travel and rehearsals.
  10. Please know this will not be an easy project but it will be a project that you will never forget. This project can change your life and we aim to build long lasting relationships and memories.
