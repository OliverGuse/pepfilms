Film Synopsis 
"Don't Stop" camper van found present day in St Louis

1970’s rock band “Don’t Stop”, three groupies along with an AWOL Soldier venture to a remote island in the Philippines. The unfortunate band of seven youth plan for peace, love, rock & roll. All the innocent youth wants is a wild time but they find a primal fight for life Everything was captured on 16mm film and found 40 years later. A documentary team relentlessly investigates the vintage found footage and the cover up. Based on true events and found footage.

Movie Plot 
The original band "Don't Stop" smoking up beside Helen's Bug

In 1972 a young up and coming teenage Filipino rock band named “Don’t Know” aspires to make it big. On their premiere gig they lose their Bassist Henry because of the lead singer “JJ’s” ego. To save the gig a 21 year old AWOL American soldier nick named “Roo” steps in and rocks the night.

The band is so impressed by his talent they offer to bring “Roo” on their trip to the remote mountainous Philippine island of Lubang. The boy’s plan to camp, party, drink, eat, smoke up, pop pills, screw and write a “hit Song”. 

Recovered photo of Jimmy, JJ, Bong, & Jobro

The newly reformed band JJ, Jobro, Boy and newest member “Roo” invite three groupies. The Debutant Princess Helena, promiscuous ex-pat Trisha and the local girl next door Olivia all think it will be good clean teenage fun.

Each of the teens has their eye on another. Its a good thing Trisha will sleep with anyone and Helena is betrothed to JJ and JJ wants to deflower Olivia and Olivia has feelings for Jobro and Boys officially likes Trisha but actually likes Jobro but Jobro likes Olivia and the new comer Roo likes Olivia too. 
Recoverd photo of Jimmy, JJ, Bong, Jobro and Raymond

In a confused spinning teenage "love heptagon" love will elude all but three. One will sacrifice and two will find each other arms just before their demise. 

JJ will not fulfil fantasy to buy his hit song, Helena never lives to accept her reluctant roll as a debutant, Boy's never inherits his family business, Jobro’s talent and song will never be known, Boy’s magic box of drugs will remain full and Roo will be deemed MIA forever till this day Roo gets over the demons of war and Olivia . The island has other plans for the unsuspecting kids and their cover up. As the kids go missing their denial transitions into prejudices that dictates the tone and tension but everything is not what is seems. Without prejudices everyone is a target.

Film Genre 

1972 - Absent Without Leave is a new type of film. Capturing a time capsule of 1972 teenage life, rock music, political views, racial tensions, and trace evidence for a modern investigation and cutting edge technology.

To nail down a Genre is impossible in the current industry standards. To best describe 1972 it is a reality, docu-horror, tragic comedy, love story, found footage, crime investigation and much more. A new type of cinema to entertain and enthrall the audience all the while in denial of the shocking 1972 events and the  

eventual horrific post traumatic out after math.  You will hate so that all the kids who died were innocent and far too young to die.

The film makers are forced to investigate the teen tragedy utilizing all the latest in technology in film restoration, sound enhancement, editing, VFXs forensic recreations, DNA, ballistics, interviews, AI polygraph, facial recognition and good old fashion gum shoe investigation. Additional, secondary long form in-depth documentary "Lost in 1972" will be produced with the mainstream film release 1972 AWOL. 

1972 - is a combination of Films and TV such as Breakfast Club, Deliverance, Almost Famous, Blair Witch Project, Rambo, Dazed and Confused, Jackass, Romeo and Juliet, Almost Famous, Apocalypse Now, Friday the 13th and Forensic Files all tied up into one original film to entertain and shock everyone. 

The film touches on deeper topics and is a time capsule of 1972 teenage life and death. Nothing is out of bounds religion, politics, peace, war, love, the future, the past, race, racism, hope, family, humor, disco, fashion, rock and roll and much more... 
In a first of its kind storytelling methodology and package that will surely define and new Genre. Be apart of this ground breaking adventure!  Enjoy a simpler time, laugh out loud, be horrified and offended all at once!
